# Skill: Abstract Signal To Concrete Spec

## Use This Skill When

A category-level signal or opportunity needs to become a concrete, actionable spec that a sourcing, product, or operations team can execute.

## Problem It Solves

This is the "so what specifically?" gap. Without it, the agent produces abstract recommendations that still need another meeting.

## Core Move

Convert an abstract opportunity into 2–3 meaningfully different concrete variants:
- de-branded generic format
- key attributes
- use-case differences
- feasibility flags

## What To Pay Attention To

- variants must be meaningfully different
- feasibility is part of concreteness
- surface the real execution blockers

## Common Failure Modes

- brand-name-as-spec
- fake variants that are just color swaps
- ignoring MOQ, lead time, regulatory, or last-mile constraints

## Typical Artifact Shape

Creates or refines:
- `sku_definitions`

## Guardrails

- a concrete spec should be actionable without a follow-up clarification meeting

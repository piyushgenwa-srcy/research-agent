# Skill: Feedback To Differentiation Opportunity

## Use This Skill When

The agent has comments, reviews, or user feedback and needs to translate that into product differentiation insight.

## Problem It Solves

Agents often summarize feedback but fail to convert it into actionable differentiation moves.

## Core Move

Turn likes, complaints, and wishlists into:
- addressable gaps
- opportunity statements
- SKU implications

## What To Pay Attention To

- distinguish product issues from logistics issues
- require repeated evidence for complaints
- focus on changes the client can actually act on

## Common Failure Modes

- treating fulfillment complaints as product opportunities
- overreacting to one-off comments
- generic feedback summaries with no decision implication

## Typical Artifact Shape

Creates or refines:
- `sentiment_opportunities`

## Guardrails

- only keep feedback that can change a spec, assortment, or differentiation decision
